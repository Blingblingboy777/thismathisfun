About Aditi

A passionate and dedicated mathematics educator, Aditi has spent years turning math from “stuck” to “solved” in creative ways. She earned her Mathematics degree in 1995 from the prestigious LSR College, and went on to complete the highly competitive Masters in Computer Applications program at Delhi University, building a strong foundation in both mathematical theory and logical problem-solving. Her professional experience includes technical and managerial roles in telecom, pharmaceutical, financial, and workforce industries.

Her journey into math education began at home, working with her own children. Determined to make math both engaging and rigorous, she developed innovative learning aids and visualization techniques that foster deeper understanding. She then started her tutoring practice with a single student, which grew organically through parent referrals and word of mouth. Today, she has worked with over forty students and hopes to continue expanding while staying true to her hands-on teaching style.

With experience teaching students from foundational elementary concepts to advanced high school coursework, she brings clarity, depth, and confidence to every lesson. Parents often share that their children enjoy her sessions, feel at ease asking questions, and connect with her enthusiasm for math. They especially value how she invites feedback, encourages participation, and makes sure ideas truly click.

Beyond the classroom, she creates engaging, easy-to-follow math content on her YouTube channel This Math Is Fun , making learning accessible to a wider audience. Her approachable explanations allow learners to revisit concepts at their own pace in true 'ThisMathisFun' style.
